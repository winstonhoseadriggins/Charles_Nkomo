Charles Nkomo

Charles Nkomo

'...I am happy to say that I now have a global audience for my work which began from humble beginnings...'

National Gallery In Bulawayo

Born and bred in Bulawayo, I did my art studies at Mzilikazi Art and Craft Centre in Bulawayo where I specialised in fine art.
After graduating, I became a resident artist at the National Art Gallery in Bulawayo where I participated in numerous solo and joint exhibitions, and also had the opportunity to get commissions to paint murals for organisations like the Zimbabwe Broadcasting Co-operation.
Over the years I have found fulfilment as an abstract expressionist.
My subject matters are influenced by the social, political and economic situation of the day while others are timeless abstracts.
I am now resident in South Africa, and I welcome invitations for exhibitions or commissions from anywhere in the world.
